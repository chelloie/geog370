After researching the safety regulations for Shearon Harris power plant, their website (linked below) said that there is a 10 mile immediate danger zone around the plant, in case of emergency.
I used the geoprocessing tool "buffer" to display this 10 mile danger zone around the plant.
Next, I wanted to see how the rivers/fish of NC could potentially be affected by an accident.
I used the geoprocessing tool "intersection" to display the intersection of major rivers/lakes and the 10 mile buffer.
This showed that 2 major rivers connected to fish spawning areas would overlap with the danger zone, therefore, there is a huge possibility that given an accident, fish populations could suffer immensely.
